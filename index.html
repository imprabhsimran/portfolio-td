"Landscapes are living systems,
not static compositions."

I am a versatile architect and landscape designer originally from New Delhi, India. I hold a Bachelor of Architecture from Jamia Millia Islamia and a Postgraduate Diploma in Advanced Project Management and Strategic Leadership from Lambton College, Ontario.

My interest in landscape architecture comes from a desire to create spaces that evolve over time — places where meaningful connections form between people and their environment. I see landscapes as living systems that respond to natural processes.

Currently, I work at Paul Sangha Creative in Canada, where I bring together architectural rigor, project management precision, and a deep sensitivity to living spaces.

Outside of work, I enjoy singing and spending time with dogs.
